About Me

I'm a dedicated clinical psychologist with a passion for creating meaningful change in people's lives. I completed my MS in Clinical Psychology from SZABIST (2024-2026), building on years of practical experience in hospital, rehabilitation, and therapeutic settings.

My approach centers on evidence-based therapy, utilizing CBT techniques, hypnotherapy, and client-centered methods to support each individual's unique journey. I believe in creating a safe, confidential environment where emotional healing can flourish.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with diverse populations, from military hospital settings to specialized rehabilitation centers. Every client brings a unique story, and I'm committed to honoring that individuality while providing professional, ethical, and compassionate mental healthcare.
